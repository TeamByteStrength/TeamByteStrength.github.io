Team Goals

Original Goals

To achieve an average of 10 working hours per student on the project.
Submit the project deliverables to the client for review and approval by November 30, 2017.
Finish filming and begin editing the raw footage by November 1, 2017.

10/12
Schedule meeting to determine filming location(s), times and group member roles.
10/26
Begin filming.
11/1
Finish filming, begin editing.
11/30
Submit project deliverables for review.

Revised Goals

For our midterm assessment, we changed to the following goals, due to the difficulty meeting with the client:

11/19
Finish filming
11/30
Deliver video

Our project’s scope changed on a fundamental level on 11-6-2017. The project’s original goal was to produce a video for use by the Boys’ and Girls’ Club. The client informed our team that it would be more helpful if we pivoted to producing stock footage and audio that future groups could use to finish the video.
